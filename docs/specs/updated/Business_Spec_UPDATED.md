# CRM-HLM — Business Spec UPDATED (Code-Aligned Rewrite)

## 1) Glossary & Definitions
### What changed and why
Original specs mixed French/English names and legacy labels (Lot vs Property, Acompte vs Dépôt vs Réservation). This rewrite adopts code-consistent canonical terms while preserving business aliases.

### Rewritten section
- **Tenant (Société)**: Top-level isolation scope. Every operational record belongs to one tenant.
- **Project**: Real-estate program under a tenant.
- **Property (Lot)**: Sellable unit tracked by lifecycle status.
- **Contact**: CRM person/legal entity; can move across prospect/client lifecycle states.
- **Deposit (Dépôt/Acompte de réservation)**: Reservation payment event tied to a contact and property.
- **Notification**: In-app user-facing event generated by domain workflows.
- **Role**: RBAC role (`ADMIN`, `MANAGER`, `AGENT`) enforcing allowed operations.

### Breaking implications
- API/UI/DB naming unchanged in code; this section is **spec-only non-breaking alignment**.

## 3) Workflows & State Machines
### What changed and why
State machines are now explicit and aligned to enums and service transitions from code.

### Rewritten section
**Contact lifecycle**
- `PROSPECT → QUALIFIED_PROSPECT | LOST`
- `QUALIFIED_PROSPECT → PROSPECT | CLIENT | LOST`
- `CLIENT → ACTIVE_CLIENT | COMPLETED_CLIENT | LOST`
- `ACTIVE_CLIENT → COMPLETED_CLIENT | LOST`
- `COMPLETED_CLIENT → REFERRAL`
- `LOST → PROSPECT`

**Property lifecycle**
- `DRAFT`, `ACTIVE`, `RESERVED`, `SOLD`, `WITHDRAWN`, `ARCHIVED`

**Deposit workflow**
- `create`: sets `PENDING`, reserves property (`ACTIVE → RESERVED`)
- `confirm`: `PENDING → CONFIRMED`
- `cancel`: `PENDING → CANCELLED`, releases property to `ACTIVE`
- `expire`: `PENDING → EXPIRED`, releases property to `ACTIVE`

### Breaking implications
- None for documented current transitions.
- If business splits deposit vs reservation, that is a **breaking domain/API change**.
