# Client Portal Guide

This guide explains how the buyer portal works from both the staff and buyer support perspective.

## 1. Portal Purpose

The portal gives a buyer controlled access to:

- their ventes
- their contracts
- their payment schedule
- related property information

It is not a general CRM access point.

## 2. How A Buyer Gets Access

1. a staff user sends an invitation or portal access from the vente workflow
2. the buyer receives a magic link
3. the buyer opens the link and starts a portal session

## 3. What The Buyer Can See

- their own deals only
- their own contracts only
- the payment details linked to those contracts
- property information tied to those records

## 4. What Staff Should Explain To Buyers

- the link is time-limited
- the session is separate from staff access
- if the session expires, a new link can be requested
- portal access is intentionally limited to buyer-owned records

## 5. Support Checklist

If a buyer reports a portal issue, confirm:

- correct email address
- correct societe
- link not already used or expired
- the underlying vente or contract actually belongs to that buyer
