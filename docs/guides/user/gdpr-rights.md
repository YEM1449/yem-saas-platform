# GDPR And Privacy Guide

This guide helps authorized users handle privacy-related operations responsibly.

## 1. What The Platform Supports

Authorized workflows include:

- export of data
- rectification-oriented review
- anonymization where legally allowed
- privacy notice access

## 2. Contact Privacy Data

Contact records may include:

- consent status
- consent date and method
- processing basis
- retention override
- anonymization timestamp

## 3. When To Export

Use export when:

- a data subject requests a copy of their data
- compliance review requires a documented extract
- internal review needs a clear picture of stored personal data

## 4. When To Anonymize

Use anonymization only when:

- the request is valid
- the business record is eligible
- the action does not violate contractual or legal retention obligations

## 5. Good Practice For Admins

- verify identity before acting
- document why the operation is being performed
- review linked contracts or legal records before erasing or anonymizing data
- involve the right compliance owner when in doubt

## 6. Important Limitation

Some records cannot be fully erased without damaging legal or contractual integrity.
In those cases, the platform follows blocking or constrained anonymization rules instead of full deletion.
